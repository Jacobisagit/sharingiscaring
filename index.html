Celtic have set up a training academy in Australia as they look to find the next Tom Rogic . The Hoops will hold an academy in Ballarat, Victoria in March and then invite a young Socceroo for a trial at the Parkhead club.Celtic already boast top Aussie midfielder Rogic and are keen to try and capture the emerging talent Down Under by sending coaches to the other side of the world for the soccer school. Celtic manager Brendan Rodgers is a big fan of Rogic and would be keen to find other potential Hoops players in the Australian market.
